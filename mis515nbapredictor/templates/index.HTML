The model score is calculated using three main variables:

  1. Win Percentage

Formula:
Model Score = (Win % × 40) + (Avg Points Scored × 0.3) - (Avg Points Allowed × 0.2) + Home Advantage

How this works:

  • Win percentage has the biggest impact, weighted by 40
  • Teams that score more points get a higher score
